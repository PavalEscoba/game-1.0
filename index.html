What's this?

This game is made for educational purposes within Rolling Scopes School course. The main purpose of this game is to give right answers for suggested questions. Questions are simple, but the further you go the more complicated tasks you get

Your character is on the left side and your enemy is on the rigth. If you click on a "Choose spell" button () you can choose a question. In case of right answer you can damage your foe, if your answer is wrong your enemy will hurt you (a bit).
